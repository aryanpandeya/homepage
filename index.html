Hi! I am Aryan

Analyst - Wealth Management Strats, Goldman Sachs

I am a red-green colorblind person, with an avid interest in movies and literature. I like to travel and write blogs about my experiences.

I was born and raised in New Delhi, India and completed my undergraduate studies majoring in Mechanical Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur. At IITK, I was associated with Vox Populi, the student journalism body. I was also coordinator for the English Literary Society, and spent a majority of my time designing and solving cryptic crosswords, word games and participating in Open Mics and JAMs. I also served as the Departmental Student Nominee for my undergraduate class.

I am currently learning French and spend my time reading literature or watching movies. I look forward to travelling across the country and the globe in the coming years, and will be documenting my experiences on my blog. I am also a rookie video-editor, making short monthly recaps of my day-to-day life. Looking forward to connect!
